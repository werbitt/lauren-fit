Lauren began her career in classical ballet, she has performed on Broadway and Internationally. After dance, Lauren has concentrated on fitness training. She is a Certified ACE Personal Trainer and was consistently a “Top One Hundred Producer in the Country” as a tier 3+ trainer for Equinox Health clubs in NYC. Some of her certifications include: Classical Pilates Instructor, Barre Instructor, Prenatal and Postpartum Personal Training, and Nutrition Counseling. Lauren believes in helping people reach their fitness goals in a healthy, pain-free way.
