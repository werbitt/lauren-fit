Lauren offers privates and semi-privates. In these classes you can get the one on one attention you are looking for. Lauren also recommends privates in some injury cases as well, so that she can design an individual program with specific modifications.

Privates and semi-privates can be provided in the following categories:

Dance

  • Ballet
  • Jazz
  • Lyrical
  • Contemporary

Fitness

  • Personal Training
  • Mat Pilates
  • Pilates Barre
  • Prenatal/Postpartum

Privates and semi-privates are open for all ages, including children. Privates can be taken at your own home or at the studio space.

For availablity and pricing please contact Lauren.
